Registration Options

There are 10 registration options.

  • Full registration is either option 1 or option 2. Each accepted paper must have a separate full registration by one of the authors (i.e. option 1 or option 2).
  • Options 3 and 4 are for attendees who are attending all 5 days. The difference from options 1 and 2 is that the student registration amount is reduced. These options for suitable for student attendees who don’t need to count their registration as a full registration.
  • Options 5, 6 and 7 are suitable for attendees only attending the conference on Monday or Tuesday.
  • Option 8 is suitable for attendees only attending the 3 day main conference and who don’t need to count their registration as a full registration.
  • Option 9 is suitable for attendees only attending for industry day.
  • Option 10 is suitable for attendees who wish to purchase an additional conference dinner ticket
Option 1(full registration): All 5 days, attending track A on Tuesday
Option 2(full registration): All 5 days, attending track B on Tuesday
Option 3(regular registration): All 5 days, attending track A on Tuesday
Option 4(regular registration): All 5 days, attending track B on Tuesday
Option 5: Monday only (workshops and tutorials)
Option 6: Tuesday track A only (Encore event and PhD forum)
Option 7: Tuesday Track B only (DAIRNET symposium)
Option 8: Wednesday-Friday only (Main conference)
Option 9: Thursday only (Industry day)
Option 10: Additional conference dinner ticket

Note: Options 1, 2, 3, 4, 8 include a conference dinner ticket (for Thursday) as part of registration. Options 5, 6, 7 and 9 do not include a conference dinner ticket.
